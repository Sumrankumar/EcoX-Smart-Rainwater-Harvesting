HH304: Campus Rainwater, Made Smart

Collect • Purify • Monitor • Reuse

EcoX is a smart rainwater harvesting and purification system for academic campuses. It automates rooftop/runoff capture, pre-filtration, sedimentation, and carbon/UF stages to deliver safe water where it’s needed.

With real-time pH, turbidity, and TDS monitoring, intelligent storage control, and usage analytics, EcoX reduces groundwater draw and operating costs—sustainably.

Features at a glance

What EcoX delivers for a sustainable campus water cycle

Automated Collection

Rooftop/runoff capture with first-flush diversion and debris removal.

Automates inlet control, first-flush bypass, and screens to keep debris out while maximizing capture during peak rainfall.

Quality Monitoring

Continuous pH, turbidity, TDS tracking with safety gating.

Sensor suite streams live pH, turbidity, TDS to the dashboard and can pause distribution if parameters exceed thresholds.

Smart Filtration

Sedimentation, activated carbon, and optional UF/UV stages.

Optimizes flow paths across settling tanks and media to extend filter life and maintain output quality automatically.

Dashboard & Control

Web controls for pumps/valves with auto/manual modes and alerts.

Control pumps and valves, acknowledge alerts, and tune automation rules from any device with role-based access.

Storage & Usage

Tank level management, overflow protection, and usage tracking.

Balances storage across tanks, prevents overflow, and tracks consumption patterns across zones and time.

Impact Analytics

Harvested volume, groundwater saved, and cost/CO₂e reductions.

Quantifies water saved, costs avoided, and emissions reductions with exportable reports for stakeholders.
